2. Nature of Our Services

My Spanish Student Visa provides administrative preparation, case management, and immigration document support services for persons seeking Spain’s student visa (estancia por estudios) and associated renewals. Our services are operational and administrative in nature and include:

  • Eligibility assessment based on published Spanish immigration law criteria
  • Document collection, review, and preparation guidance
  • Coordination of official sworn translations of required documents
  • Preparation and review of application forms and supporting materials
  • Case management and progress communication via our client dashboard
  • Liaison and guidance during the consulate submission and processing period

Our services do not include Spanish legal advice, tax advice, financial advice, or any professional regulated service under UK law. We do not act as solicitors, regulated immigration advisers, or financial advisers. We are immigration specialists and case workers providing administrative and document preparation support.

3. Immigration Advice — UK Regulatory Position

Important: Retail Consulting UK Ltd is not regulated by the Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner (OISC). Our services relate exclusively to applications for Spain’s student visa under Spanish immigration law. The OISC regulatory framework applies to advice and services relating to UK immigration matters only. As our services concern applications to Spanish (non-UK) immigration authorities, they fall outside the scope of OISC regulation.

We are not solicitors and we do not provide legal advice. The information on this website is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Nothing on this website creates a solicitor-client relationship between you and Retail Consulting UK Ltd.

If you require formal legal advice in relation to UK immigration law or any other regulated legal matter, you should consult a solicitor or regulated legal professional.

4. Our Legal Partner — Platinum Legal Spain

Immigration case work and legal representation within Spain is carried out in collaboration with Platinum Legal Spain, a Spanish legal services firm operating under Spanish law and regulated accordingly in Spain. Platinum Legal Spain is a separately constituted entity and is not a trading name of Retail Consulting UK Ltd.

Where your case requires formal legal representation before Spanish immigration authorities, this is provided by Platinum Legal Spain. Retail Consulting UK Ltd provides the administrative preparation and case coordination service; Platinum Legal Spain provides the legal representation and submission in Spain.

References to “our legal team” or “immigration lawyers” on this website in the context of Spanish immigration proceedings refer to the lawyers at Platinum Legal Spain acting in their capacity as our legal partner for Spanish immigration matters.

5. No Guarantee of Outcome

Immigration decisions are made solely by the relevant Spanish government authority — the Spanish consulate responsible for the applicant’s jurisdiction. My Spanish Student Visa and Platinum Legal Spain have no influence over the outcome of any application. We cannot guarantee approval of any application, regardless of the quality of the case presented.

Approval depends on factors including the applicant’s eligibility at the time of submission, the completeness and accuracy of the documentation provided, and the discretion of the relevant authority. Our fee structure — in which the final stage is payable only on approval — reflects the outcome-linked nature of our service, but does not constitute a guarantee of approval.
