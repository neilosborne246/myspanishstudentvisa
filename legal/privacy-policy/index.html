Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR, the EU GDPR, and Spain’s data protection law (LOPDGDD).

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Retail Consulting UK Ltd, Company No. 06711242, trading as My Spanish Student Visa.

Immigration legal services are delivered in partnership with Platinum Legal Spain.

Contact: hola@myspanishvisa.com

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Information you provide when submitting an enquiry or application
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) collected via analytics
  • Communications you send to us by email or contact form

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you request
  • Process your visa application in partnership with Platinum Legal Spain
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve and monitor the performance of our website
  • Send service-related communications (we will not send marketing emails without your consent)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases: performance of a contract (where you have requested our services), compliance with a legal obligation, and our legitimate interests in operating our business and improving our services.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your data with Platinum Legal Spain for the purpose of delivering immigration legal services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g. email platforms, payment processors) under appropriate data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Immigration case files may be retained for up to 6 years in line with professional legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request erasure (“right to be forgotten”), restrict or object to processing, and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hola@myspanishvisa.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority — the ICO in the UK (ico.org.uk) or the AEPD in Spain (aepd.es).

8. Cookies

We use cookies as described in our Cookies Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified on this page with an updated date.
