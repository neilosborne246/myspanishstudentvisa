Common Questions

Before you get in touch

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

Email enquiries are responded to within one working day. If you start an application through the dashboard, a case manager is assigned within one working day and available to message immediately after that.

Do I need to book a formal consultation before applying?

No. You can start your application directly through the dashboard and your free eligibility check will confirm everything before you commit. If you have specific questions beforehand, email us at hola@myspanishvisa.com and we’ll give you honest guidance on your situation.

I’m not sure if I qualify — can you check before I pay?

Yes. The eligibility check is free and happens before you pay Stage 1. We review your course, institution, nationality, and finances. If anything prevents you from qualifying, we tell you at this stage rather than after you’ve paid.

Which nationalities do you help?

We specialise in applications from the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Ireland, South Africa, and New Zealand. Applicants from other non-EU countries are also welcome — email us to confirm whether we can assist with your specific consulate.

Can I contact you about renewing my existing student visa?

Yes. We help with both new Spain student visa applications and annual renewals (prórroga de estancia por estudios). Start your enquiry through the dashboard or email us directly.
