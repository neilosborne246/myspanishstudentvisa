Common Questions

Before you get in touch

Is the eligibility quiz really free?

Yes, completely free. You create an account on the dashboard, answer a short set of questions about your course, nationality, institution, and finances, and we confirm whether you qualify — before you pay anything. There’s no obligation to proceed.

What happens on the free call?

One of our immigration specialists will go over your specific situation — your course, your consulate, your timeline, and any questions you have. It’s a genuine conversation, not a sales call. You can book it through the dashboard after creating your free account.

How quickly will you respond to my email?

Within one working day. If you need a faster answer, starting your application through the dashboard gets you a case manager assigned within one working day who you can message directly any time.

I’m not sure if I qualify — can you check before I pay?

That’s exactly what the free eligibility quiz is for. We review everything — course, institution, nationality, finances — and flag any issues before Stage 1 payment. If you don’t qualify, we tell you upfront.

Can I contact you about renewing my existing student visa?

Yes. We help with both new applications and annual renewals (prórroga de estancia por estudios). Log in to your dashboard if you’re an existing client, or email us at hola@myspanishvisa.com to discuss a renewal.
