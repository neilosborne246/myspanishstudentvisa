Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026  ·  My Spanish Student Visa / Retail Consulting UK Ltd

This cookie policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use on myspanishstudentvisa.com, and how you can manage your preferences. For broader information on how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them when you return or browse between pages. Cookies can be first-party (set by us) or third-party (set by external services). They may be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored for a set period).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly (essential cookies)
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (analytics cookies)
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising on platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google Ads (marketing cookies)
  • Remember your cookie preferences (preference cookies)

4. Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use session recording or heatmap cookies (e.g. Hotjar), live chat widget cookies, or cookies set by embedded third-party social media widgets.

5. Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit this website, you will be presented with a cookie notice where you can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some features of this website.

  • You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • You can manage Google ad personalisation settings via Google Ads Settings
  • You can manage Meta ad preferences via Meta Ad Preferences

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
