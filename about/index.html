Who We Are

A Single-Focus Spain Student Visa Service

My Spanish Student Visa exists for one reason: to take the complexity out of applying for a Spain student visa. We are not a general immigration agency trying to serve every visa type. We focus exclusively on the Spain student visa — the estancia por estudios — for non-EU nationals from English-speaking countries.

Our immigration specialists and case workers bring together deep expertise in Spanish immigration requirements, consulate-specific knowledge, and the hands-on document experience that comes from managing hundreds of applications. We understand the differences between the Miami consulate and the London consulate, between an ACRO certificate and an FBI check, between a qualifying health insurance policy and one that will be rejected at the window.

My Spanish Student Visa is backed by Platinum Legal Spain — a Spanish immigration practice with established operations. All casework is supervised by qualified professionals and delivered through a 24/7 online client dashboard, giving you full visibility of your application at every stage.

What We Do

How We Help You Get Your Spain Student Visa

Our service covers every stage of the process — from your first eligibility check to your TIE card on arrival in Spain.

🔍

Free Eligibility Check

Before you pay anything, we review your course, institution, nationality, and financial situation. If anything needs adjusting, we tell you upfront — not after months of wasted preparation.

📋

Personalised Document Checklist

Every client receives a bespoke checklist based on their nationality, consulate, and course — with country-specific instructions for criminal records, apostilles, and sworn translations.

✍️

Application Form Preparation

We prepare your EX-00 form in Spanish, correctly completed, with every field aligned to your enrolment letter and accommodation proof. No English form errors.

🏥

Health Insurance Arranged

We connect you with a student-compliant Spanish health insurance policy through our partner network — no co-payments, no deductibles, accepted at all Spanish consulates.

🏛️

Consulate Submission Support

We review your complete application pack before your consulate appointment and remain available to handle any queries the consulate raises after submission.

🪦

Post-Arrival Guidance

Once your visa is approved and you arrive in Spain, we guide you through empadronamiento, your TIE card appointment, and the first-month administrative steps.
