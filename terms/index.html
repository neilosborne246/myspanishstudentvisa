2. Our Services

My Spanish Student Visa provides end-to-end preparation and support services for Spain’s student visa (estancia por estudios), both for new applications and renewals. Our services include:

  • Eligibility assessment and guidance
  • Document review, preparation, and collection guidance
  • Official sworn translation of required documents (up to the stated value per application type, included in the Stage 2 payment)
  • Preparation and review of the complete application pack
  • Case management via our client dashboard
  • Liaison with Spanish immigration authorities (Spanish consulate) and Platinum Legal Spain
  • Support through to application decision

Our services do not include: government consulate fees (~€80), private health insurance premiums, apostille fees, or translation costs above the stated included value. See our Pricing page for full details.
